Ingredients in the jar:

  • 250 g of flour
  • 125 g of sugar
  • 125 g of almond powder
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon

To complete the recipe, you will need to add:

  • 125 g of soft butter
  • 1 egg
  • Icing sugar (for sprinkling)

Preparation:

  2. Add all the dry ingredients from the jar to a bowl: flour, sugar, almond powder, and cinnamon.
  3. Add the egg and soft butter. Knead the dough until homogeneous.
  4. Form a ball with the dough and let it rest in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes.
  5. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the dough to a thickness of about 5 mm.
  6. Using various cookie cutters, cut out the biscuits and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  7. Bake for about 10-12 minutes, or until the biscuits are lightly golden.
  8. Let the biscuits cool on a rack.
  9. Sprinkle the cooled biscuits with icing sugar.
